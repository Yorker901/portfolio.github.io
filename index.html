StayConnect

"StayConnect" addresses the high churn rates faced by telecommunications companies by developing a machine learning model to predict the likelihood of customer churn. Using supervised learning techniques, the project trained a binary classification model with algorithms such as Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting on customer data. The model achieved high accuracy, precision, and recall, enabling the company to identify at-risk customers and implement targeted retention strategies. As a result, the company was able to reduce churn rates, increase revenue, and enhance customer satisfaction.
